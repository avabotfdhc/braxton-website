Meet Braxton

"Hi! I'm Braxton, and I'm 8 years old. I started B's Wild And Wonder because I noticed something: kids' clothes were always falling apart, and the good stuff was way too expensive. So I fixed it."

I wanted to create clothes that could keep up with my adventures—climbing trees, building forts, exploring the woods—and I wanted other kids to be able to afford them too.
