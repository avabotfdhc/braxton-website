Meet Braxton

Hi! I'm Braxton, and I'm 8 years old. I started B's Wild And Wonder because I noticed something: kids' clothes were always falling apart, and the good stuff was way too expensive.

I wanted to create clothes that could keep up with my adventures—climbing trees, building forts, exploring the woods—and I wanted other kids to be able to afford them too.

Built by Braxton. Tested by kids. Every product is kid-approved because I'm the first one to try them out!
