Returns & Exchanges

Hassle-free returns because we want you to love your purchase

Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase! If you're not satisfied for any reason, we offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery.

Return Requirements

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Tags must still be attached
  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Original packaging is preferred but not required

How to Return

1

Contact Us

Email us at hello@bwildandwonder.com or use our contact form to start your return. Include your order number and reason for return.

2

Get Your Label

We'll send you a prepaid return label via email. Print it out and attach it to your package.

3

Ship It Back

Drop off your package at any USPS location or schedule a pickup. We'll cover the return shipping cost!

4

Get Refunded

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We recommend placing a new order for the item you want and returning the original item for a refund. This is the fastest way to get what you need!

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us right away with photos. We'll send you a replacement or issue a full refund—your choice. We cover all shipping costs for damaged or defective items.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags attached
  • Items returned after 30 days
  • Gift cards

Refund Information

Refunds will be issued to your original payment method. Please allow 3-5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement after we process it. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

30-Day Returns

Not happy? Return it within 30 days for a full refund. No questions asked!

Free Return Shipping

We provide prepaid return labels for all US orders. Returns are on us!

Need Help?

Questions about returns? We're here to help!
