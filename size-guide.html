How to Measure

  • Height: Have your child stand against a wall without shoes. Mark the top of their head and measure from the floor to the mark.
  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of the chest, keeping the tape measure level.
  • Waist: Measure around the natural waistline, where the waistband of pants would sit.
  • Head Circumference: Measure around the widest part of the head, just above the eyebrows and ears.

Pro Tip: If your child is between sizes, we recommend sizing up for longer wear. Our clothes are designed to be comfortable and allow for growth!
