Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about delivery

Shipping Options

🚚 Standard Shipping (US)

Cost: $5.99 (FREE on orders over $50!)

Delivery Time: 5-7 business days

Our most popular option! Orders are processed within 1-2 business days and delivered via USPS or UPS.

⚡ Express Shipping (US)

Cost: $12.99

Delivery Time: 2-3 business days

Need it faster? Choose express shipping at checkout for quicker delivery.

🇨🇦 Shipping to Canada

Cost: $9.99 (FREE on orders over $75!)

Delivery Time: 7-14 business days

We ship to Canada via USPS International. Please note that customs fees and import duties may apply and are the responsibility of the buyer.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays). You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is placed, and a shipping notification with tracking information once your order ships.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website. If you have any issues with tracking, don't hesitate to contact us.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We'll work with the carrier to locate your package or send a replacement.

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship to the US and Canada. We're working on expanding to more countries soon! Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when we add new shipping destinations.

Free Shipping Threshold

US orders over $50 ship FREE!

Canada orders over $75 ship FREE!
