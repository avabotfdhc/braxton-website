The story of an 8-year-old entrepreneur with a big dream

Hi, I'm Braxton!

I'm 8 years old, and I love adventures. Whether I'm climbing trees in my backyard, building forts in the woods, or camping with my family, I'm always exploring and discovering new things.

But I noticed something that bugged me: my clothes were always falling apart! And when my parents tried to buy me better quality stuff, it was super expensive. That didn't seem fair.

So I had an idea: What if I created my own clothing line? Clothes that could keep up with kids like me—climbing, running, jumping, exploring—and that didn't cost a fortune?

And that's how B's Wild And Wonder was born!

The Problem I Noticed

Have you ever had a favorite shirt that got holes in it after just a few washes? Or pants that ripped the first time you climbed a tree? Yeah, me too.

And then there's the price problem. The really good outdoor clothes cost way too much. I wanted to fix both of those problems.

My Solution

I work with suppliers to create durable, comfortable clothes that can handle real kid adventures. And because I run this business efficiently, I can keep prices fair.

Every single product is tested by me first. If I wouldn't wear it on my own adventures, I won't sell it to you.

Quality Control: The Braxton Test

Here's how I make sure every product is adventure-ready

🧗

Climb Test

I climb trees, playground equipment, and rocks. If the clothes restrict movement or rip, they don't pass.

🏃

Run Test

I run, jump, and play tag. The clothes need to keep up without riding up, falling down, or getting in the way.

🌧️

Weather Test

I test jackets and outerwear in real conditions. They need to keep kids dry and comfortable.

🧺

Wash Test

I wash everything multiple times. Colors should stay bright, and fabric should stay soft and strong.

😴

Comfort Test

If it's not comfortable enough to sleep in (after a long day of adventures), it's not good enough.

👍

Kid Approval

I have my friends test everything too. It needs the Braxton AND the friend stamp of approval!

Giving Back

Helping others live their best life—let's do it together!

I believe every kid should get to experience the magic of the outdoors. That's why a portion of every sale goes to organizations that help kids get outside and explore.

We support:

  • Youth camping programs
  • Outdoor education initiatives
  • Programs that provide adventure gear to kids in need

When you buy from B's Wild And Wonder, you're not just getting great clothes—you're helping another kid have their own adventure.
