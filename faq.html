Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Braxton is an 8-year-old entrepreneur who founded B's Wild And Wonder! He started the company because he noticed that kids' clothes were always falling apart and the good stuff was too expensive. He tests every product himself to make sure it can handle real kid adventures.

Braxton started B's Wild And Wonder after getting frustrated with clothes that couldn't keep up with his adventures. He wanted to create durable, comfortable clothing that kids could actually afford. With help from his family, he turned his idea into a real business!

We carry sizes for kids ages 0-12! Our clothing ranges from 0-3 months up to size 12 youth. Each product page has a detailed size chart to help you find the perfect fit. When in doubt, we recommend sizing up—kids grow fast!

Each product page has a size chart with measurements. We recommend measuring your child's chest, waist, and height, then comparing to our chart. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for longer wear. Still unsure? Contact us and we'll help!

We use high-quality, durable materials that can handle active play. Most of our clothing is made from soft cotton blends, with some performance fabrics for activewear. All materials are tested by Braxton for comfort and durability!

Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days in the US. We also offer expedited shipping options at checkout. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships!

Yes! We ship to Canada. Canadian orders typically take 7-14 business days. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees and import duties, which are the responsibility of the buyer.

We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Simply contact us to start your return, and we'll provide a prepaid return label.

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website. You can also contact us if you need help tracking your order!

We stand behind our products! If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us right away with photos. We'll send you a replacement or issue a full refund—your choice.

Yes! We offer gift wrapping for a small additional fee. You can select this option at checkout. Each gift-wrapped item comes in our signature adventure-themed packaging with a personalized note card.

We process orders quickly, but if you contact us within 2 hours of placing your order, we'll do our best to make changes or cancel it. After that, we may not be able to intercept the package, but you can always return items once received.

A portion of every sale goes to organizations that help kids experience the outdoors. We support youth camping programs, outdoor education initiatives, and programs that provide adventure gear to kids in need. When you shop with us, you're helping another kid have their own adventure!

We partner with various youth-focused outdoor organizations. Currently, we support local camping scholarships, outdoor education programs in schools, and gear donation programs. We're always looking for new partners—contact us if you know of a great organization!

Most items can be machine washed in cold water and tumble dried on low. We recommend washing inside out to protect prints and colors. For specific care instructions, check the tag on your item or the product page.
